About Us

Hello there and welcome to Aga's Bakery, the one stop shop for all of your vegan cake needs!

As a passionate vegan Aga was never completley satisfied with the limited selection of tasty cakes and treats available. Having always had a love of baking she decided to craft her own recipes.

Finally after years of refining those recipes (as well as eating the mistakes) she took the decision to share her creations.

All cakes from Aga's Bakery are handmade and 100% vegan friendly although if you are not vegan they are equally as delicious.

So grab yourself a tasty treat to enjoy and thank you for stopping by.
